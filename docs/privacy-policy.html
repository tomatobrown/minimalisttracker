Privacy Policy for End of Day

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

End of Day ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Daily Check-in Responses: Your answers to daily questions, including yes/no responses and numerical data you enter.
  • Custom Questions: Any custom questions you create within the app.
  • Monthly Challenges: Information about challenges you set and your progress toward them.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Basic device information required for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve user experience.
  • Subscription Information: Purchase and subscription status managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Store your daily check-in data locally on your device
  • Display trends and statistics based on your responses
  • Process subscription purchases and manage your access
  • Send you daily reminder notifications (if you enable them)
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage

Local Storage: All of your daily check-in responses, questions, and challenges are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Cloud Services: We use third-party services for specific features:

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and in-app purchases. RevenueCat collects anonymous user identifiers and purchase information necessary to manage subscriptions.
  • Apple App Store: Processes all payments and manages subscription billing through your Apple ID.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (anonymous user ID and purchase data only)
  • Apple: For payment processing and subscription management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible through the app.
  • Delete Your Data: You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data.
  • Export Your Data: Use the app's export feature to download your data.
  • Cancel Your Subscription: Manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

End of Day is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminder notifications, we will send you local notifications on your device at your specified time. You can disable these notifications at any time through the app settings or your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: Apple Privacy Policy
